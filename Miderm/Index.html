Here's a little about myself~

Hi, welcome to my website, I coded it myself! I'm a CT/NY based, multi-facited performer finally getting her feet wet in the business. When I was 5 I got to be in my first musical, Charlie and the Chololate Factory and was cast as the leader of the line of child Oompa Loompa's and right when I walked onto the stage my first show, I was hooked. I've been doing it constantly since then, even though I try to culminate other hobbies. I love museums, painting, being a cat mom, and teaching children's theatre: anything that makes me more worldly of a person since I think that aspect is an important part to being an actor. Anyway, feel free to explore and contact me~ Thank you!
